About 2FA Shield

2FA Shield was engineered out of the clear necessity for a highly performant, security-first authenticator platform that does not force complex storage schemes, accounts, or telemetry mechanisms upon its users.

Cryptographic Foundation

The calculation layer executes strictly following RFC 6238 time-based standard parameters via the highly-performant, auditable OTPAuth.js core engine executing directly within your modern browser window runtime framework.

Why Zero Storage?

Storing credentials anywhere, even locally encrypted, poses risk layers. By performing exclusively inside volatile runtime system memory allocation space, your underlying seed generation tokens remain clean of continuous storage exposure vectors.
