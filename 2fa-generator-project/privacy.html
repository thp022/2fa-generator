Privacy Policy

Your absolute security and explicit privacy form the design limits of our architecture. No configurations are compromised to collect insights.

Data Processing & Zero-Transmission Guarantee

  • No Networks: Your Base32 keys never leave device sandboxed execution space.
  • No Analytics: There are no tracking tools, telemetry layers, error beacons, or metrics collection systems embedded.
  • No Persistence: We explicitly refuse the inclusion of Cookies, LocalStorage, or SessionStorage tracking tokens. Everything vanishes on tab closure.

Camera Stream Isolation

The visual QR engine stream operates strictly via local canvas pixel analysis loops. Frames are processed entirely locally and instantly discarded after extracting valid cryptographic configuration structures.
